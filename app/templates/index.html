About SafemamaPikin

At SafemamaPikin, we believe no woman should lose her life while giving life. By combining cutting-edge Artificial Intelligence with a compassionate network of human volunteers, we bridge the critical gap between rural communities and essential maternal healthcare. Our mission is simple: safer pregnancies, healthier babies, and empowered mothers across Nigeria.

Key Features

Automated Reminders

AI-powered voice calls remind patients of upcoming appointments in their local language, reducing no-show rates.

Volunteer Escalation

A dedicated queue for volunteers to handle complex queries, failed calls, and follow-ups efficiently.

Data Management

Securely manage patient and volunteer data with role-based access controls and location-specific privacy.

Advanced Analytics

An intelligent dashboard and reporting system to track project impact, call outcomes, and operational metrics.

Multilingual Support

Breaking language barriers with support for Hausa, Igbo, Yoruba, and Pidgin, ensuring every mother is heard.

Secure Donations

A transparent, secure platform for donors to fund medical supplies and emergency transport directly.
